Who it's built for

One platform.
Two types of operations.

🤝
Freight Brokers
For independent brokers and small brokerages doing $500K–$20M in freight annually who are tired of managing their business across 6 different tools.
  • Manage loads, carriers, and customers in one place
  • Track gross profit per load and per customer
  • Send rate cons and get digital carrier acceptance
  • Give shippers a portal to track their freight
  • Build your pipeline with the built-in CRM
🚛
Asset Carriers
For small to mid-size carriers with their own trucks and drivers who need a dispatch system without the enterprise price tag.
  • Dispatch your own drivers from a live board
  • Track loads from pickup to delivery
  • Manage driver check calls and ETAs
  • Invoice customers and track receivables
  • Monitor carrier insurance and compliance

The story
"Freight brokers and carriers deserve software built for the way they actually work — not the way enterprise vendors think they work."

FreightFlowHQ was built out of a simple frustration: the logistics software market is either enterprise tools that cost tens of thousands of dollars, or dated platforms with terrible interfaces that haven't been updated since 2008.

We built FreightFlowHQ from the ground up for the way modern logistics businesses actually operate — combining TMS, CRM, dispatch, and financial tools into one platform that's fast, intuitive, and priced for the companies that need it most.
